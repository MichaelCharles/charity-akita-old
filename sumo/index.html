About

Every Spring for the past 12 years a charity sumo tournament has been held in Akita City. Every year the proceeds from the tournament go to a charitable cause. This year's the event's proceeds will be donated to Mirai no Mori. Please sign up for our mailing list if you'd like to be notified immediately of any updates.

We invite you to join us this spring at the 13th Akita International Sumo Tournament. Every year we have wrestlers of every shape, size, and experience level, so if you're interested, please sign up to compete! If competing isn't really your style but you'd like to help out, let us know that you would like to volunteer.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact Charity Akita at CharityAkita@gmail.com. You can also sign up to receive updates about the sumo tournament.

Volunteer

If you are interested in volunteering, please contact us and let us know how you'd like to help.

The Akita International Sumo Tournament is an event which can only take place due to the help and support of volunteers. Every year we need judges, an announcer, door attendants, and a score keeper. We would also be appreciate of anyone willing to take pictures and video of the event.

Compete

All who wish to participate should sign up using this form providing your real name, your wrestler name, your hometown, where you live currently, and your wrestler slogan.

All are welcome to join no matter skill level or current residence. This year we will again be having both a men's and a women's division. The Akita International Sumo Tournament is a rare opportunity for women to participate in this part of Japanese culture. Every year we have participants from all over Japan with varying skill levels.

If you are interseted in participating but have no experience with sumo have no fear! We will be providing basic lessons in the rules of sumo. If you are particularly worried, Owen Cunningham will provide an introductory lesson on sumo on a date prior to the actual event. The time and location of the lesson has yet to be announced. If you are interseted, please sign up for updates.
