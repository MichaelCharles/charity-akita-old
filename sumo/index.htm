About

Every Spring for the past 12 years a charity sumo tournament has been held in Akita City. Every year the proceeds from the tournament go to a charitable cause. This year's charity has not yet been chosen. Please sign up for our mailing list if you'd like to be notified immediately of any updates.

We invite you to join us this spring at the 13th Akita International Sumo Tournament. Every year we have wrestlers of every shape, size, and experience level, so if you're interested, please sign up to compete! If competing isn't really your style but you'd like to help out, let us know that you would like to volunteer"

If you have any questions, feel free to contact Charity Akita at CharityAkita@gmail.com. You can also sign up to receive updates about the sumo tournament.

Attend

When

We are still awaiting confirmation on the date from the budoukan. The event will be held on either the first or second weekend of June.

Doors open at 11:30am.

Where

The event will be held at the Akita Kenritsu Budōkan (秋田県立武道館).

Cost

Spectators     1000円
Middle school and high school students     500円
Elementary school students and younger     FREE

Volunteers are wrestlers will receive a 500円 discount.
