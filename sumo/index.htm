About

Every Spring for the past 12 years a charity sumo tournament has been held in Akita City. Every year the proceeds from the tournament go to a charitable cause. This year's charity has not yet been chosen. Please sign up for our mailing list if you'd like to be notified immediately of any updates. will be held.

We invite you to join us this spring at the 13th Akita International Sumo Tournament. Every year we have wrestlers of every shape, size, and experience level, so if you're interested, please sign up to compete! If competing isn't really you're style but you'd like to help out, let us know that you would like to volunteer"

If you have any questions, feel free to contact Charity Akita at CharityAkita@gmail.com.
