About

Every Spring for the past 12 years a charity sumo tournament has been held in Akita City. Every year the proceeds from the tournament go to a charitable cause. This year's charity has not yet been chosen. Please sign up for our mailing list if you'd like to be notified immediately of any updates. will be held.
