Charity Akita

  • 日本語

Charity Akita is a charity fundraising and volunteer organization comprised of ALTs and residents of Akita who organize fundraiser events for charities, create events to welcome newcomers into Akita prefecture, and otherwise seek to work towards grassroots internationalization.
