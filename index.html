Charity Akita

  • 日本語

Charity Akita is a charity fundraising and volunteer organization comprised of ALTs and residents of Akita who strive to create connections between foreign expats and the wider Japanese community. We organize fundraising events for charities, create events to welcome newcomers into Akita prefecture, and otherwise seek to work towards grassroots internationalization by forming meaningful relationships.

Announcement!

On June 13th, 2016 the executive committee of Akita Association of JETs passed a charter which among other things, has renamed the organization to "Charity Akita." Currently we are in a transitional phase. We thank you for your patience.
