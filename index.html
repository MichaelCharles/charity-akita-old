Welcome

Akita Association of JETs (AAJ) (秋田ＪＥＴ参加者の会) is a team of Akita community members who seek to create connections between foreign expats and the wider Japanese community. We organize fundraising events for charities, create events to welcome newcomers into Akita prefecture, and otherwise seek to work towards grassroots internationalization by forming meaningful relationships. For more information, contact the AAJ team at AkitaAAJ@gmail.com.
