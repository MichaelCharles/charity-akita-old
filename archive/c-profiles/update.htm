Updates on this Morning's Election Problems

Election polls will reopen at 1:00pm today (2015/07/23). The polls went up at 9:07am. I received a message informing me that Chey's profile was not displaying correctly at 9:20am. The candidate profile pages and the election poll itself were pulled from the website the first chance I got, around 9:40am. By that time three votes had been entered.

The election poll was reset at 11:50am. All users who voted before then will need to vote again. Seeing as Chey's position was not being displayed in the best way possible, in the interest of being fair, I reset the poll. This means that all votes entered up to that point were deleted.

What happened? Why was Chey's profile displaying wrong? All of the profiles started as this template page. Chey's candidate profile still had some of the Greek filler text. This was just a mistake on my part. When I copy and pasted her campaign essay, I must've forgot to delete part of the template text.

Was this really such a big deal? Probably not. And it is an easy thing to fix. But when I received the message that something was messing up, I was in the middle of giving interview tests and I had no time to look at it properly. I assumed the worst and pulled the election materials the first chance I got.

9:07am? Why not 9:00am? WordPress has a post scheduling system. Since I was in interview tests, I was relying on that to make the post. Something about it wasn't set up correctly, and I had to manually log in and submit the post. I'm still not 100% sure why it didn't work, but I think it might be because WordPress treated the post as a draft and not as something ready to be published. I'll look into this when I have more time.
