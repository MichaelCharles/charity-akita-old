AAJ Halloween Party 2015

日本語の情報はFacebookページであります。

The annual AAJ Halloween Party is only a week away! Come to Jamhouse in Akita City on Saturday, October 31 wearing your best costumes! Entry is 2,500 yen or 2,000 yen with your student ID. Proceeds will be donated to Blue for Tohoku, an NPO which works to help and support children living in orphanages in Tohoku.

You will also be able to purchase raffle tickets for 100 yen. Half of the money raised by the raffle will be given to the winner, and the other half donated to AkitaFrog (あきた冒険遊びfrog), a local children’s play park.

For more information about the event, please see our Facebook page.
