2015 AAJ Farewell Beach Party

It’s time for one more big bash before we say goodbye to some of our lovely friends. Come out for a great party and camping!

We will be partying it up at Shimohama Beach. Orange House has allowed us to use their facilities for this event.

Cost is ¥500

We are going to have a BBQ during the evening. AAJ will provide the charcoal and fire starters and Orange Bar has generously lend us their grills. Please bring your own food.

AAJ will be running a bar for people to get drinks. You can purchase 3 drink tickets for ￥1000. Orange Bar will also be selling drink if you don’t like AAJs options. Please remember that this event is at venue and bringing your own drinks is not allowed.

You can pitch your tent behind Orange bar on the beach or if you do not have a tent you can set your sleeping bag up inside orange bar after the party.

Lastly some of us will be going to the beach early to have a fun beach day. Orange Bar has offered us a HUGE dissent on rentals. They have jet skis, boars, banana boats, and wake boards. Please come down early for fun time.
