2015 AAJ Committee Member Elections

Elections are underway! In order to vote, you will need to have a Charity Akita account. In order to prevent people from creating multiple accounts, these accounts are created by the Charity Akita admin upon request. If you do not have a Charity Akita account, please go to this link in order to request one. Be advised that account creation approval could take up to 24 hours, so don’t wait until the last minute to register!

Be sure to read the Candidate Profiles before casting your vote.

Candidate Profiles

Candidates for Chairperson

  • Chey Parlato
  • Alana Stephenson

Candidates for Vice-Chairperson

  • Maeleen Arce
  • Michael Aubrey

Candidates for Secretary

  • None

Candidates for Treasurer

  • Joey Griggs

Candidates for PR Manager

  • Rachel Youngblood

VOTING IS CLOSED.

The poll opened on Wednesday July 23, and will close on Monday July 27 at 10pm. Results will be posted on Tuesday July 28 at 10am.

You must vote by the deadline. There will be no exceptions. Please request a Charity Akita account well before the polls close in order to ensure that you will get a chance to have your say.
