AAJ Financial Records

Hey, everyone. Today AAJ would like to announce something to you that we have been working on the past two months:* our newly organized, fully transparent collection of AAJ Financial Records. Here you can access a number of files that will give you a better idea about how we operate and raise money for charities within the prefecture and across the country. Read on for more details.

AAJ Event Financial Reports

This first group of documents is great if you are looking for just a quick snapshot of the financial details of any one event. In the “AAJ Event Financial Reports” folder you will find be able to find simple overviews of any AAJ event, including: date, location, attendance, revenue, expenditures, and charity/donation details.

We suspect that for most people these summaries present almost exactly what you’ll want to know about a given AAJ event, but we’ve got more information we can share with you if you are looking for more details.

Event Expenditures and Receipts

The second group of files can be found in the “Event Expenditures and Receipts” folder. This folder contains a separate folder for each AAJ event, which in turn contains a spreadsheet which is used to calculate the total cost of said event. Alongside the spreadsheet, you will be able to find scans of all of the receipts from that event, including those for supplies, the venue, transportation of equipment, and any other associated costs.

Bank Account

The third group of files that you might be interested in are those in the “Bank Account” folder. Here you can find digital scans of the AAJ bank book, showing you exactly what was in the account at any given time, along with details about what goes in and what comes out.

Along with these scans is an easier to view spreadsheet which lists the exact same information as the bank book, but with greater detail about each transaction.

Donation Receipts

Finally, you can take a look at the last folder, “Donation Receipts”, to confirm that all of the money raised from our events is actually making its way to the promised charities. It’s as simple as that: this folder is a repository for scans of all receipts received after we make our donations.

We hope these documents have helped you understand AAJ better and give you confidence that every yen we bring in is used exactly how was promised. All of these documents will be updated regularly (within a week of any transaction is our goal), and we will do our best to fill in information from before the new team took over if it is available. If you have any suggestions on how to improve our records, please feel free to pass them along. We’d also be happy to answer any questions you have about our finances at any time.

 

*This has been just about complete for a little more than a month now, but we wanted to wait until the Welcome Party money had been donated before putting up this post (the charity didn’t get their furikomi info to us until last week).

2014-2015 Akita Association of JETs Financial Report

The following is a list of the events hosted by AAJ in the 2014-2015 year, the individual event proceeds (in yen) and the charities they were donated to. The financial year for AAJ begins with the Welcome Party, and ends with the Farewell Party.

Welcome Party 146,611 Misonotenshien Orphanage
Lake Tazawa Camping 32,000 Misonotenshien Orphanage
Halloween Party 464,000 Peace Winds Japan
Soup Curry Quiz 22,500 Japan Cat Network
 Caroling 57,654 Toys & Books for Akita Orphanages
Appi Ski Trip N/A N/A
Valentine’s Masquerade 5,000 Japan Red Cross
St. Patrick’s Party 73,000 Japan Red Cross
Hanami Tea Party N/A N/A
International Sumo Tournament 80,156 Save the Children Japan
Farewell Party 10,000 Equal Marriage Alliance Japan

*A small amount of proceeds were also raised/donated via the AAJ Book Exchange for the Japan Red Cross

The total value of proceeds donated during the 2014-2015 JET year was 914,310円.

At this time, all the proceeds raised in the 2014-2015 year have been donated to the charities specified above, and all money owed to AAJ has been paid in full. Below is the exact value donated to each charity as verified by the donation receipts on record from last year.

  • Akita Misonotenshien Orphanage: 200,000円
  • Akita Misonotenshien Orphanage (Toys & Books): 57,654円
  • Peace Winds Japan: 464,000円
  • Japan Cat Network: 22,500円
  • Japan Red Cross: 80,000円
  • Save the Children: 80,156円
  • Equal Marriage Alliance (EMA) Japan: 10,000円

A note about the 2014-2015 year:

Unfortunately, the records from last year are not exactly clear about the total amount of proceeds made at each event (the documents we have show some conflicting information), but the numbers do match up fairly well with the values shown on the donation receipts. The numbers in the table above are believed to be generally correct; however, one notable exception is that 200,000円 was donated to the Misonotenshien Orphanage (confirmed by the receipt), but our records show only 178,611円 was raised from the Welcome Party & Tazawako Camping. We don’t have a clear record to confirm exactly where the extra donation money came from, but fortunately, from this point onward, our new record keeping procedures that will prevent this confusion from arising again.

For any further information about past AAJ events and proceeds, please check the Akita JET Wiki,  http://akitajet.com/wiki/AAJ#Annual_Events

Thank you all for your generosity and support in the 2014-2015 year!
