Halloween Poster Contest WINNER!

Akita Association of JETs is excited to announce the winner of our first ever Halloween Poster Contest, Jeffrey Lowther. His creative and spooky Sugichi will make an excellent attraction for this year’s Halloween Party. Congratulations Jeff and thank you so much for your submission!

Also, a big thanks to everyone who submitted a poster creation and to those who took the time to visit the site and vote. We appreciate your participation and support!

We can’t wait to see everyone at this year’s Halloween Party on October 31st!
